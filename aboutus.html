ABOUT Us

AFIRST LIFESCIENCE PVT. LTD. is a part of the four decade-old group with a global presence engaged in developing and manufacturing pharmaceutical valuable products that address unmet medical needs since 1976. The group had gained immense importance and carved a niche for itself in the pharmaceutical industry with a view to explore new avenues in the export market.

AFIRST LIFESCIENCE PVT. LTD. was registered in the year 1987 and then commenced its export to global market with full swing. The company has a deep understanding of pharmaceutical manufacturing process and its strength lies in the deep knowledge in export related activities which has helped the company to create a reputed name and position in the market wherever it launched its operations.

Our Mission

Our endeavour is to fortify the medical care being provided in all over through quality products manufactured with excellent technology with GMP certified facilities.

Core Values

Ethics, professionalism, integrity, quality of service, innovation and trust are excellent core values. These values are implemented in every aspect of the company combined with our deep understanding of the market.

Board Of Directors

Our overall management and supervision is undertaken by the Directors. Our day-to-day management is the responsibility of the Directors. The company has a team of experienced and professional executives who carry on their responsibilities diligently.

Under the stewardship of C.E.O., we have grown in turnover, profits and business reputation.
